## Key Notes

### What is in the SRD vs. what is not
- The SRD contains ONLY spells from the **PHB (Player's Handbook)** core rulebook
- Domain spells that are unique to domains (e.g., Chaos Hammer, Holy Smite, Order's Wrath, Unholy Blight) are also SRD
- Spells from **Spell Compendium**, **Complete Arcane**, **Complete Divine**, **Complete Mage**, **Book of Exalted Deeds**, **Book of Vile Darkness**, and other supplements are NOT in the SRD
- The SRD also does NOT include spells from setting-specific books (Eberron, Forgotten Realms, etc.)

### Approximate Unique Spell Count
- Sorcerer/Wizard: ~400 spells across 10 levels (0-9)
- Cleric: ~250 spells across 10 levels (0-9)
- Druid: ~200 spells across 10 levels (0-9)
- Bard: ~180 spells across 7 levels (0-6)
- Paladin: ~45 spells across 4 levels (1-4)
- Ranger: ~50 spells across 4 levels (1-4)
- Many spells are shared across multiple class lists
- Total unique spells in the SRD: estimated ~450-500
